What is Cultivate?

Cultivate is the app that helps you learn how to garden on your land, no experience needed! We do the guesswork for you, calculating what plants are best using your unique plot's yearly sun exposure, average rainfall and climate.

How does it work?

Locate

We'll find your average sun exposure and rainfall for you.

Learn

We'll suggest plants and give you instructions on how to care for them.

Grow

Get out there and plant!
